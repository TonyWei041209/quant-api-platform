# Daily Research Workflow Guide

## How to Use This Platform Every Day

### Morning Routine (2-3 minutes)

1. **Open Dashboard** — your Daily Research Home shows:
   - Data freshness and instrument count
   - Upcoming earnings events
   - Platform health and DQ status
   - Your watchlists
   - Recent activity feed
   - "Continue Where You Left Off" with saved presets and notes

2. **Check your watchlists** — see which instruments need attention today

3. **Review recent activity** — pick up where you left off yesterday

### Research Session

1. **Go to Research Workbench**
2. **Select universe** — choose "All Instruments" or a specific watchlist
3. **Run analysis** — Summary, Performance, Valuation, or Drawdown
4. **Save preset** — save your current research config for reuse
5. **Save note** — capture your thesis, observation, or risk assessment
6. **Run as Backtest** — test your idea with historical data

### Backtest Workflow

1. **Configure backtest** — strategy, tickers, dates, slippage
2. **Run and review** — check return, Sharpe, max drawdown
3. **Save note** — record your interpretation of results
4. **Compare runs** — view past backtests on Dashboard

### Execution (Controlled)

1. **Create intent** — from research or backtest results
2. **Generate draft** — from approved intent
3. **Review risk** — check risk assessment
4. **Approve** — manual approval required
5. **Submit** — live submission locked by default (FEATURE_T212_LIVE_SUBMIT=false)

### Key Features

| Feature | Purpose | Where |
|---------|---------|-------|
| **Watchlists** | Daily focus universe | Dashboard, Research |
| **Saved Presets** | Reuse research configs | Research, Dashboard |
| **Research Notes** | Capture thesis/observations | Research, Dashboard |
| **Recent Activity** | Resume work context | Dashboard |
| **Daily Brief** | Today's research context | Dashboard |

### Tips for Building a Daily Habit

- Create a "Daily Focus" watchlist with 5-10 instruments
- Save your most-used screener as a preset
- Write a brief note after each research session
- Check the Dashboard first thing each morning
- Use presets to avoid re-entering parameters
